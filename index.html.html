why do i choose BSIT

I chose BSIT because ever since I was in high school, I was inspired by watching my cousin do coding and programming.
That’s why I wanted to take an I.T. course However, after graduating from high school and moving into senior high school,
my mind changed I started wanting to pursue nursing instead.

But when I graduated from senior high school, my mom couldn’t afford to send me to a nursing course.
That’s why I went back to my original plan and chose the I.T. course.
If you ask me whether I regret taking BSIT, my answer is no because I.T. was actually my first choice from the beginning.

Another reason I chose BSIT is because of the opportunities it offers.
We live in a digital age where technology plays a vital role in almost every industry.
From healthcare and business to education and entertainment, IT professionals are in demand.
By taking this course, I know I am preparing myself for a career that is both stable and full of possibilities for growth and innovation.

Lastly, I chose BSIT because I enjoy learning how to solve problems.
Programming challenges me to think critically and creatively.
It’s not always easy, but the satisfaction I get from making a program work or fixing an error motivates me to keep learning and improving.
